Water Quality Lab Analysis

According to JJM Guidelines, each lab is required to test at least 3000 samples per financial year

Different Water Quality Labs and samples tested by them till FY 2024-25

Water Testing Analysis:

  • A total of 9416 water samples were tested in Mysuru District.
  • The Mysore lab has a higher capacity for testing water quality parameters as it is a district-level lab. But still its testing is less than Heggadedevana(HD Kote) lab which is a sub-district lab.
  • Heggadedevana Lab has shown better performance.
  • Krishnarajanagar lab has not passed the 3000 testing threshold.
  • Optimizing sample flows to the lowest cost labs could increase savings in the testing budget.
