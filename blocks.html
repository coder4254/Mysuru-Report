Mapping of Water Quality Parameters

This is the blockwise mapping of each parameter for Mysuru district. The analysis covers 16 parameters. Please note that some parameters might not be available due to a lack of samples tested and collected for them.

The median has been used as a metric to plot the graphs by integrating multiple samples from multiple sources in a village.

The distribution is analysed for Pre-Monsoon (April-June) and Post-Monsoon (October-December) for each block.
